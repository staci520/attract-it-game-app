Set a personal goal and increase your magnetism to it by focusing your thoughts, emotions, imagination and actions. The Goal and Feeling Statement is a simple, two-sentence statement of what you want and how you want to feel when you get it.
Step 1

Consider what you want? What is your goal? If you could be granted one wish, what would you ask for?

Step 2

Choose a single, clear and concrete personal goal you believe is possible. You can turn an affirmation into a goal or take a situation in your life that you want changed and reverse it into a positively worded goal.

Step 3

How would you feel if you had already achieved your goal? This will be the feeling that you want your goal to give you during gameplay. Choose at least one feeling, but no more than four.

Step 4

Write a simple, two-sentence statement of what you want (Goal) and how you want to feel when you get it (Feeling). You will repeatedly repeat this Goal and Feeling Statement during gameplay.

Step 5

Use the present tense, as if your goal and feelings are already accomplished facts: I am now/I now have...(Goal) I feel ...(Feelings).

Step 6

Perfect your Goal and Feeling Statement. Play with the wording until it clicks. Two sentences: one for the goal and one for the feeling the goal will give; word it in the present tense, positive and clear and concise.
